# Value Proposition (Free Beta)

Core promise
- Save hours of market research by aggregating prices, news, sentiment, and picks in one place.
- Turn market noise into actionable ideas with clear signals and risk context.

What users get today (free)
- AI picks for day/swing/long timeframes.
- Hidden Gems and Quick Wins for idea generation.
- News Bombs and stock-specific news analysis.
- Social sentiment and market overview.
- Watchlist sync and alerts.
- Mobile-friendly experience with fast load + cached data.

Why it is valuable
- Fewer tabs and less manual scanning.
- Faster decision-making with clear prioritization.
- Consistent signal framing (confidence, risk, catalysts).

Positioning
- "Your one dashboard for research, signals, and actionable picks."
- Built to save time, not replace judgment.

Future premium (when users are active)
- Advanced alerts (email/SMS, custom rules).
- Exports and reports.
- Historical performance / backtest views.
- Power-user controls (filters, custom universes).
